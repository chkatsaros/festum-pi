Spectral Theory of Graphs 2024

Brief Summary: We will study the spectral properties of the adjacency and the Laplacian operator on graphs. Using these, we will present combinatorial solutions to the heat equation of graphs. The goal will be to use the calculations to get information on the combinatorial and connectivity properties of graphs. As a final application, we will apply our calculations to Cayley graphs of groups and use them to derive combinatorial information on the underlying group.
