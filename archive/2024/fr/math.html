Mathematics summer school and conference 2024

About: The mathematical part of this third edition of Festum Pi will be composed of a summer school (July 8th-14th) followed by an international conference (July 15th-20th). Both events will be focused on recent developments in analysis and mathematical physics: leading experts will deliver lectures about kinetic and fluid equations, elliptic and parabolic systems, crowd motion, optimal transport, resonances, etc.
The summer school will gather around 45 graduate students and postdocs, who will attend 5 mini-courses, each divided into three 1.5 hours sessions. The blend of Greek students and international students will foster a genuine atmosphere of integration. Students are encouraged to stay for the international conference, which will consist of around fifteen 1 hour lectures. A lot of free time will be left to participants, in order to foster scientific discussions and the emergence of new collaborations.

Location: The summer school and the conference will both take place at the Mediterranean Agronomic Institute of Chania, located in Chania / La Canée (Crete).

Accommodation and meals: Accommodation and meals will also be provided at the Mediterranean Agronomic Institute of Chania, and are taken care of by the local organizers. Meals are organic, with choices for vegans and vegetarians.

Directions: The conference center is located 3 km south-east of Chania city centre, 13 km from the airport and 1 km from the port of Souda. See here for more information.

Practical details: Both blackboard and slides talks are welcome. Electrical outlets are the same as in France. Wireless Wifi is provided inside the buildings.

Arrival and departure: Participants of the summer school will arrive on July 8th and, if they are not staying at the conference, they will leave on July 14th. Participants of the international conference, if not already present during the summer school, will arrive on July 14th and leave on July 20th.
